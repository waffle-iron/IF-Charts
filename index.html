IF-Charts - What is it?

IF-Charts gives you accurate plates for your favourite airports, complete with SID, STAR and IAP plates. Make sure to request charts if your favourite airport is not available at the moment!

  • Get IF-Charts on all devices

    IF-Charts will work well, so you can use it anywhere you go, at home or away.

  • All the charts

    You'll get all the charts for an airport that are publicly available, including SID, STAR and IAP charts.
